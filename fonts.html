International Phonetic Alphabet

How to type it

Typing the IPA requires a special font that includes the required letters and diacritics, and a keyboard layout that will give you access to all those symbols.

Fonts

  • LaserIPA is $99.95, so for my purposes (just for fun, for now at least) this isn't practical.
  • Duolos is one of the few free IPA fonts provided by SIL.
  • Gentium is another SIL font.
  • Charis SIL, as you can guess by the name, is yet another SIL-provided font. I'm beginning to like these SIL guys!
